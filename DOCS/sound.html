2.2.2. Audio output devices

  • OSS (ioctl) driver
  • SDL driver (supports up/downsampling, esd, arts etc)
  • native ALSA 0.5 driver
  • native ALSA 0.9 driver (buggy, use OSS emulation!)
  • ESD (through SDL)
  • ARTS (through SDL)
  • SUN audio driver (/dev/audio) for BSD and Solaris8 users
